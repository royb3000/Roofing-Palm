Shingle Options

Types of Asphalt Shingles

We offer a full range of asphalt shingle styles to match your home's architecture, budget, and performance requirements.

3-Tab Shingles

The classic, time-tested choice for budget-conscious homeowners. 3-tab shingles feature a flat, uniform appearance with a clean, traditional look. They are lightweight, easy to install, and provide reliable protection at the most affordable price point. Ideal for rental properties, starter homes, and straightforward roof designs.

✓ Most affordable option
✓ 15-20 year lifespan

Architectural Shingles

Also known as dimensional or laminated shingles, architectural shingles are our most popular recommendation for South Florida homes. Their multi-layered construction creates a rich, textured appearance that mimics the look of natural wood or slate. They offer superior wind resistance and a longer warranty than 3-tab options.

✓ Best value for performance
✓ 25-30 year lifespan

Designer Shingles

For homeowners seeking the ultimate in curb appeal and performance, designer shingles deliver premium aesthetics that replicate the look of natural slate, cedar shake, or old-world tile. These heavyweight shingles are engineered for maximum wind and impact resistance, making them an excellent choice for high-end Florida properties.

✓ Premium curb appeal
✓ 30+ year lifespan

FAQ

Shingle Roofing Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about asphalt shingle roofing in South Florida.

The cost of a shingle roof varies based on your home's size, roof pitch and complexity, the shingle grade you choose, and any necessary structural work. 3-tab shingles are the most budget-friendly, while architectural and designer shingles cost more but offer significantly better performance and aesthetics. We recommend scheduling a free inspection so we can provide you with an accurate, itemized estimate tailored to your specific home and needs.
In South Florida's intense heat and humidity, the lifespan of asphalt shingles depends on the quality of the product and installation. Standard 3-tab shingles typically last 15 to 20 years, while high-quality architectural shingles can last 25 to 30 years with proper maintenance. Designer and premium shingles may exceed 30 years. Factors like ventilation, UV exposure, and storm frequency can also affect longevity. Regular inspections help catch and address small issues before they become costly repairs.
We work with the industry's top manufacturers known for producing shingles that perform well in hot, humid, and hurricane-prone environments. GAF, Owens Corning, and CertainTeed are among the most trusted names, each offering product lines specifically engineered for high-wind and high-heat regions. As a certified installer for these brands, Roofing Palm can offer enhanced warranty coverage that goes beyond standard manufacturer guarantees. During your consultation, we will help you select the best brand and product line for your home's specific requirements.
Palm Beach County falls within the High-Velocity Hurricane Zone (HVHZ), which requires roofing materials to meet some of the strictest wind resistance standards in the country. Shingles installed in this area must be rated for wind speeds of at least 110 mph, though many of the architectural and designer products we install are rated for 130 mph or higher. All of our installations comply with the Florida Building Code and local municipal requirements, and we ensure proper fastening techniques and underlayment systems are used to maximize wind performance.
