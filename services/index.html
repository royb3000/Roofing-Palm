For South Florida, we most commonly recommend metal roofing, concrete tile, clay tile, or high-quality architectural shingles. Metal roofs offer excellent hurricane resistance and superior energy efficiency, while tile roofs provide outstanding durability in coastal environments and a classic aesthetic. Architectural shingles offer an excellent balance of performance and value. During your consultation, we evaluate your property, budget, and style preferences to help you select the ideal material for your specific situation.
Our free inspection will determine the best course of action for your roof. Generally, if your roof is under 15 years old with only localized damage such as a few missing shingles or a small leak, targeted repairs may be sufficient and more cost-effective. However, if your roof is aging, shows widespread deterioration, has multiple leaks, or exhibits signs of structural compromise, a full replacement is often the smarter long-term investment. We always recommend the most economical solution first and provide transparent options so you can make an informed decision.
