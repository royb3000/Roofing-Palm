Why It Matters

Waterproofing Is Essential in South Florida

South Florida's subtropical climate creates one of the most demanding environments for building exteriors in the entire country. With annual rainfall exceeding 60 inches, a hurricane season that spans six months, and humidity levels that routinely surpass 80%, every surface of your property faces relentless moisture assault.

Without professional waterproofing, water intrusion leads to structural deterioration, mold growth, interior damage, and a dramatic decrease in your property's value. The cost of preventative waterproofing is a fraction of what remediation and repair can run after water damage takes hold.

At Roofing Palm, our waterproofing systems are specifically formulated for the Palm Beach County climate. We use premium coatings and membranes that resist UV degradation, flex with thermal cycling, and create an impenetrable barrier against even the most punishing tropical downpours.

Our Expertise

Types of Waterproofing We Offer

From rooftops to foundations, we deliver full-spectrum waterproofing protection for residential and commercial properties across Palm Beach County.

☂

Roof Waterproofing

Advanced membrane and coating systems that seal your roof against driving rain, ponding water, and wind-driven moisture. Ideal for flat and low-slope roofs common across South Florida.

☶

Deck Waterproofing

Protect outdoor living spaces with durable, slip-resistant waterproof coatings that stand up to pool splash, afternoon storms, and the intense Florida sun without cracking or peeling.

⛨

Foundation Waterproofing

Below-grade barrier systems that prevent groundwater intrusion and protect your home's structural integrity. Especially critical for properties near the high water tables common in South Florida.

⚖

Balcony Waterproofing

Multi-layer waterproofing for condominium and residential balconies that prevents water from penetrating through to the units below while maintaining an attractive finish surface.

♺

Parking Structure

Traffic-bearing membrane systems designed for parking decks and garages. Engineered to withstand heavy vehicle loads, chemical exposure, and the constant moisture of the coastal climate.

⚋

Coating Systems

High-performance elastomeric and silicone coatings that provide seamless, energy-efficient waterproofing for existing structures. Extend the life of your roof and walls by decades.

How We Work

Our Waterproofing Process

Every waterproofing project begins with a thorough inspection using moisture detection equipment and thermal imaging to identify vulnerable areas and existing water intrusion points. We never guess where the problems are -- we find them with precision.

Our certified technicians then prepare the surface with professional-grade cleaning, crack repair, and priming before applying the selected waterproofing system. We use only top-tier products from manufacturers like Tremco, Henry, and GAF that are proven in the South Florida environment.

After application, we conduct comprehensive quality checks including flood testing on flat surfaces and adhesion testing on vertical applications. Every project is backed by our workmanship warranty and the manufacturer's material warranty, giving you complete peace of mind.

The lifespan of a waterproofing system depends on the type of product used and the surface it's applied to. High-quality elastomeric and silicone roof coatings typically last 10-20 years in South Florida's demanding climate. Below-grade foundation membranes can last the lifetime of the structure. We select products specifically rated for UV exposure, thermal cycling, and the moisture levels found in Palm Beach County, and every installation comes with both a workmanship warranty and a manufacturer material warranty.
Common warning signs include water stains on ceilings or walls, peeling paint or bubbling drywall, musty odors indicating hidden mold, damp or wet spots in the basement or ground floor, efflorescence (white mineral deposits) on concrete surfaces, and cracked or deteriorating sealant around windows, doors, and expansion joints.
