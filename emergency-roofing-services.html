Be Prepared

What to Do in a Roofing Emergency

Follow these four critical steps to protect your home and family while you wait for our emergency crew to arrive.

1

Ensure Safety First

Move your family and pets away from the damaged area. If water is entering through the ceiling, stay clear of saturated drywall that could collapse. Turn off electricity to affected areas if safe to do so.

2

Call Roofing Palm

Contact us immediately at (561) 473-0192. Our emergency dispatch team is available 24/7 and will deploy a crew to your location within 60 minutes of your call.

3

Contain the Damage

Place buckets under active leaks and move furniture, electronics, and valuables away from water. If you have tarps available, carefully cover exposed areas from inside, but never climb onto a damaged roof yourself.

4

Document Everything

Take photos and videos of all visible damage before any cleanup. This documentation is critical for your insurance claim. Our team will also perform a thorough assessment and create professional documentation for your insurer.

Emergency Services

Types of Emergency Roof Repairs

Our emergency crews are equipped and trained to handle every type of roofing crisis across South Florida.

🌪

Storm Damage Repair

South Florida's severe thunderstorms and hurricanes can rip off shingles, dislodge tiles, and tear through roofing materials. Our emergency team rapidly assesses storm damage, secures exposed areas, and begins permanent repairs to restore your roof's integrity.

💧

Leak Repair

A sudden roof leak can cause thousands of dollars in water damage within hours. We locate the source of the leak quickly using advanced detection methods, stop the water intrusion immediately, and perform lasting repairs to prevent recurrence.

⛳

Tarping Services

When immediate permanent repair isn't possible, professional tarping is essential. We install heavy-duty, hurricane-rated tarps that are properly secured to withstand continued wind and rain, buying you time until full restoration can be completed.

🌳

Tree Damage Repair

Fallen trees and large branches can cause catastrophic roof damage, compromising structural supports and puncturing roofing materials. Our crew safely removes debris, evaluates structural integrity, and repairs or replaces damaged sections.

🌀

Wind Damage Repair

High winds can lift, crack, or completely remove roofing materials, especially at edges and ridges. We specialize in wind damage assessment and repair, re-securing loose materials and replacing damaged sections to restore full weather protection.

🌊

Flood Damage Repair

Heavy rainfall and flooding can compromise flat roofs, overwhelm drainage systems, and cause ponding that leads to structural damage. We address flood-related roofing issues, restore proper drainage, and reinforce vulnerable areas against future events.
