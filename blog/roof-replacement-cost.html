How Much Does a Roof Replacement Cost in West Palm Beach?

Everything you need to know about roof replacement pricing, materials, permits, and financing in South Florida.

A roof replacement is one of the most significant home improvement investments you will make. In West Palm Beach, where roofs endure relentless sun, saltwater air, and hurricane-force winds, choosing the right materials and contractor is critical. Here is a comprehensive breakdown of what a roof replacement costs in 2024 so you can plan and budget with confidence.

Cost by Roofing Material

The roofing material you choose is the single largest factor in your total replacement cost. Here is what West Palm Beach homeowners can expect to pay per roofing square (100 square feet), fully installed:

  • 3-tab asphalt shingles: $350 to $500 per square. The most affordable option with a 15 to 20-year lifespan. Suitable for budget-conscious homeowners but less wind-resistant than architectural shingles.
  • Architectural (dimensional) shingles: $450 to $700 per square. Our most recommended shingle option for West Palm Beach, offering enhanced wind resistance (up to 130 mph), a 25 to 30-year lifespan, and a more attractive dimensional appearance.
  • Metal roofing (standing seam): $800 to $1,400 per square. Premium durability with a 40 to 70-year lifespan. Excellent energy efficiency and hurricane resistance. Increasingly popular across Palm Beach County.
  • Concrete tile: $600 to $1,000 per square. Outstanding durability and fire resistance with a 40 to 50-year lifespan. A staple of South Florida architecture.
  • Clay tile: $1,000 to $1,800 per square. The premium choice for Mediterranean and Spanish-style homes. Exceptional longevity of 50 to 100 years with proper maintenance.
  • Flat roof (TPO/modified bitumen): $400 to $800 per square. Ideal for flat and low-slope commercial and residential roofs common in modern South Florida design.

Total Cost by Home Size

To estimate your total replacement cost, multiply the per-square price by the number of roofing squares your home requires. Most West Palm Beach homes fall between 15 and 30 squares. Here are typical total costs for an average 2,000-square-foot home (approximately 20 to 25 roofing squares):

  • Asphalt shingles: $8,000 to $15,000
  • Metal roofing: $18,000 to $35,000
  • Concrete tile: $14,000 to $25,000
  • Clay tile: $22,000 to $45,000
  • Flat roof: $9,000 to $18,000

Permits and Code Requirements

Roof replacements in West Palm Beach require a building permit from Palm Beach County. This is not optional -- it is a legal requirement that protects you as the homeowner. Permit costs typically range from $200 to $500 depending on the scope of work. Your contractor should handle the entire permitting process on your behalf.

Florida Building Code also mandates specific requirements for new roofs in our wind zone, including:

  • Minimum wind uplift resistance ratings based on your location within the county
  • Secondary water barrier (peel-and-stick underlayment) on the entire roof deck
  • Enhanced fastening schedules for shingles and tiles in high-velocity hurricane zones
  • Impact-resistant requirements in certain areas near the coast

Additional Costs to Consider

Beyond the base material and labor costs, several additional expenses may apply to your roof replacement project:

  • Tear-off and disposal: $100 to $175 per square for removing the old roofing material. Required in most cases.
  • Decking repair: $50 to $100 per sheet of plywood if rotted or damaged decking is discovered during tear-off
  • Fascia and soffit repair: $500 to $2,500 depending on the extent of damage
  • Upgraded underlayment: $50 to $100 per square for premium synthetic underlayment over standard felt paper
  • New gutters: $1,500 to $4,000 if your gutter system needs replacement at the same time
  • Skylight replacement or resealing: $300 to $1,500 per skylight

Financing Options

  • 0% interest for 18 months: Extended no-interest period for larger projects
